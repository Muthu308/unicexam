About UNIC Academy

Started during Navarathiri 2012 with just 4 students in Tirunelveli. Today, UNIC Academy has guided 1000+ students in GATE, IIT JAM, JEE, NEET, Engineering and Science subjects.

Vision

World-class knowledge with strong moral values that empower students in academic, professional and personal lives.

Mission

  • Outcome-based education with employability focus
  • Build confidence through competitive exam training
  • Create lifelong learners adaptable to new technologies
Why UNIC?
  • Faculty from IITs, NITs and reputed institutions
  • Personal mentorship and individual attention
  • Periodic evaluations and progress tracking
  • Basics to advanced level coverage
  • Previous Year Questions (PYQ) practice and shortcut techniques
  • Timely syllabus completion
